About

I am an Assistant Professor at the Centre for Quantum Science and Technology (CQST), IIIT Hyderabad. My research interests lie in quantum information theory, quantum foundations, and statistical physics. I enjoy exploring the fundamental limits of nature’s computation and information processing capabilities.

Prior to joining IIIT Hyderabad, I held postdoctoral positions at the University of Gdansk and the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research. I received my Ph.D. in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Mohali.

I am always happy to work with motivated students interested in exploring the theoretical frontiers of quantum science.
