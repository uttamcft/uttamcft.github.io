About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Centre for Quantum Science and Technology (CQST), IIIT Hyderabad. My broad research interests lie at the intersection of quantum information theory and quantum thermodynamics. I am particularly interested in resource theories, quantum estimation, quantum correlations, and quantum foundations.

Education

  • PhD in Physics, Harish-Chandra Research Institute, Allahabad
  • MSc in Physics, University of Hyderabad
  • BSc in Physics, University of Delhi

Postdoctoral Experience

  • Postdoctoral Fellow at Institute of Theoretical Physics, University of Warsaw
  • Postdoctoral Fellow at International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS-TIFR), Bengaluru

Research Interests

  • Quantum Resource Theories
  • Quantum Thermodynamics
  • Quantum Information and Estimation Theory
  • Quantum Foundations
