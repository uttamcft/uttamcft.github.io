About

I have a deep interest in the mathematical aspects of quantum information theory and computation. I am particularly fascinated by their potential to shed light on foundational challenges such as the quantum measurement problem and quantum thermodynamics. Moreover, I aspire to leverage these mathematical tools to explore and develop energy-efficient quantum technologies, aiming to contribute to both fundamental understanding and practical advancements in the field.
